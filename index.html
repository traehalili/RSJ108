How to Create a Podcast

Assembly time: 2 days
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

It's easy to create a podcast and get a couple episodes out there. You just need an idea and some equipment and you're ready to have fun!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Laptop or desk computer
  • Microphone
  • Headphones
  • A pop filter and mic stand
  • Sound mixing board
  • Quiet Room

  1. Choose a podcast topic that you're passionate about

    Your best bet is to create a podcast that covers material you're interested in and excited to talk about. Try listening to other podcasts for inspiration.

  2. Put in preparation before recording

    You can easily go unscripted and press record. However, it helps to put in research and preparation before the you start recording. Having at least a basic outline of the topics you're covering helps with the flow of your show.

    Think about what you want to say for each episode

  3. Gather the necessary recording hardware for your podcast

    You could record and create a podcast with nothing but a smartphone. However, to make quality content it is helpful to have the necessary equipment for recording. Keep in mind that you don't need to break the bank when you first start recording for your podcast.

  4. Find an editing software and a podcast hosting service

    There are many editing softwares to choose from like Hindenburg and Digitial Audio Workstation. There are also many podcast sites where your audience can listen from. Do your research and look at reviews to find the best options that work best for you.

  5. Build your audience

    Let your audience know that you want to engage with them. Ask them for questions and comments and give your audience a way to get in touch with you. Social media is the best way to connect with your audience, so be sure to create an account on many platforms for your podcast.
